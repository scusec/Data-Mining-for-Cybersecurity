Our mission is to design and develop a bionic artificial liver for the treatment of liver failure. To achieve this goal, we first need to understand how the liver works. In the liver, factors such as flow, structure, and tissue properties can influence the uneven distribution of chemical composition and consequently result in the anisotropic cellular functions. However, it is difficult to identify the effects of such factors because we lack appropriate tool-sets. We specialize in the design and development of ORGANS ON CHIPS for biological, medical, and clinical research. We manufacture microsystems using innovative processes in microscience and bioengineering. Through studying the activity of liver cells under microenvironments, we will understand liver cell physiology at a fundamental level and provide insight into the design and development of a bionic artificial liver.
